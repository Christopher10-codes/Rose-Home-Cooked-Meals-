Our Story

Rose's Home Cooked Meals began with a simple passion for creating delicious, hearty meals that bring people together. What started as a small home kitchen has grown into a professional catering service trusted by thousands across the Eastern Cape.

We take pride in maintaining the warmth and authenticity of home-cooked meals while delivering the professionalism and reliability expected from top-tier caterers. Our team is dedicated to making your event memorable through exceptional food and service.
